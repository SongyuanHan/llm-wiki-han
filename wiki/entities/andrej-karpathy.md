# Andrej Karpathy

> AI researcher, educator, and former Tesla AI Director; author of the LLM Wiki pattern.

## Overview

Andrej Karpathy is a prominent AI researcher and educator known for his work in deep learning and computer vision. He was a founding member of OpenAI, served as Director of AI at Tesla (where he led the Autopilot vision team), and later returned to OpenAI. He is widely known for his educational content — detailed blog posts, YouTube lectures on neural networks, and hands-on coding tutorials that have taught a generation of ML engineers.

In April 2026, he published the [[llm-wiki-pattern]] as a GitHub gist — a manifesto describing how LLMs can be used to build and maintain persistent personal knowledge bases. The gist quickly gained over 5,000 stars and 3,000 forks, spawning numerous community implementations.

## Key Contributions

- The [[llm-wiki-pattern]] — a pattern for LLM-maintained personal knowledge bases
- Educational content on neural networks, transformers, and deep learning
- Leadership of Tesla's Autopilot computer vision team
- Advocacy for "vibe coding" and LLM-assisted development
